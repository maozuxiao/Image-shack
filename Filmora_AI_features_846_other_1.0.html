AI Copilot Editing

AI Copilot Editing

If you are getting used to Filmora or started your video editing journey in the last while, you must master the AI Copilot feature. AI Copilot is a personal assistant designed to assist users throughout video editing. This AI chatbot can provide insights into improving your video and directions to perform the task. With its multi-lingual support, it comprehends a vast range of data in different languages and answers accordingly.

This feature will not only help you master Filmora, but it will also uplift your editing skills. Without further delay, let’s explore more about AI Copilot and how it helps in work management:

Access Personal AI Assistant

After launching Filmora, once you create a new project in it, you'll be directed towards Filmora's main editing interface. To start asking questions, suggestions, and directions related to Filmora from this assistant, navigate toward the timeline toolbar and find the "AI Copilot" option towards your right. Click this option, and Filmora's dedicated personal AI assistant will appear on your screen.

Adjust Parameters Specifically According to Your Footage

With this AI's natural language comprehension and broad training input, it can answer specific questions related to editing features. It helps in finding the exact functions so that users don't have to waste their time roaming around Filmora's interface. They can stay dedicated to the task and edit effortlessly. If you are using the free version, you can still ask AI Copilot for up to 50 questions related to these parameters.

Access Buttons Relevant to Questions

Let's accept that only some people are good with directions, and if you are one of them, Filmora also has a solution. After answering your question, there is a button at the bottom of the answer leading to that feature or functionality. For instance, in the first example above, the AI Copilot will provide an "Add Transition" button to access the relative function directly.

 

AI Music Generator

AI Music Generator

Incorporating music into video projects is an obvious thing but legal rights of the content cause difficulty. If you use Wondershare Filmora for video editing and compelling music, then you might be aware of its AI music generator feature. It offers copyright-free music using AI algorithms and enables you to make your videos commercially available.

This feature will assist you with compelling, unique music that perfectly matches your projects. So, let’s discover an AI music generator in detail for quickly getting original music.

Launch and Access AI Music

Launch Filmora after successfully downloading it and start a new project from its main interface. Enter in the main editing panel, navigate towards the "Audio" section in the custom toolbar, and expand the AI Music option. Under the tool section, click on "Start" and find various moods and themes for the AI audio of your choice.

Set Preference and Generate Music According to Your Video

Select preferences such as "Mood" and "Duration" that you want in your AI music. There are eight moods ranging from happy, sad, exciting, chill, epic, peaceful, hopeful, and emotional. Choose an audio theme from five available options: vlog, cinematic, dance, travel, and sport.

Customize music duration and generate music of 20 seconds to 10 minutes that align with your video length. Specify the number of tracks you wish to generate from 1 to 10 numbers, and click on "Start" to get the music right there.

Download or Directly Add Music in Filmora Projects

Based on your liking, get a music preview generated on your right side within the same panel. If you are satisfied with the results, then hit the "Download" icon and directly add it to your Filmora project. Moreover, you can either look at the "Generated" folder to get already generated music.

AI Text-Based Editing

AI Text-Based Editing

Adding a subtitle to a video was a hectic and time-consuming task in the past. No more with Filmora 13! Now, Filmora AI can automatically detect the voice dialogue in videos and edit the video with text directions. Using the AI Text Clipper tool in Filmora, users can quickly remove unwanted portions from video. It is the same as Silence Detection, which can improve the efficacy of video editing.

This feature is here to assist, especially in the case of adding and modifying text in the video. Without wasting further time, let’s learn how we can utilize the AI Text-Based and Sound-Based Editing features to the maximum.

Access the AI Text Clipper Option

Check out the official website of Wondershare Filmora and ensure that you have downloaded the latest version of Filmora 13. Get the "AI Text Clipper" from the home screen or right-click on your video on the timeline and explore that option from the list. You will get two options: Sound-Based Editing and Text-Based Editing.

Remove the Selected Text with Text-Based Editing

Discover "Text-based Editing" and select the video language for transcription. Click "OK" to navigate toward another screen. There, you will find a preview section, text, and timeline section below.

From the text section, select and remove any unwanted text that will automatically be deleted from the video as well. You can search the text by using the search bar above and delete the text one by one. To delete all content from the video, use the "Delete all silent clips" button.

Insert and Modify Subtitles

If you want to add or modify subtitles, check the "Subtitle Editor" option below the timeline. Later, click on "Export to Timeline," and your video will start showing subtitles layers.

To edit your subtitles, double-click on the subtitle layer and lead into another window. Now, you can adjust and change the subtitle to define the font size, font alignment, and letter spacing. Adjust the font styles from the font presets, customize color, add an outline, and include a background to your text. Additionally, apply multiple animations to your subtitles.

AI Thumbnail Crea

AI Thumbnail Crea

Attractive video thumbnails are essential to make the audience curious to watch the video. However, creating a thumbnail is a time-consuming process and requires various software to get an engaging thumbnail. Wondershare Filmora’s newer version makes this task simpler with its AI Thumbnail Editor feature. It has the ability to recognize the best screenshot from the video and contains other editing tools for perfect thumbnails.

Now, let's delve into details and learn the process of creating thumbnails with Filmora AI Thumbnail Editors. By knowing that you will be able to instantly create an eye-catching thumbnail of video:

Setup and Access the AI Thumbnail Editor in Filmora V13

Firstly, ensure that you have the latest version of Filmora, which is V13, that is available on its official website for free. Then, double-tap and start editing your project in Filmora. After that, proceed to the "Export" button and navigate to another window. In the next window of export, click on the "Edit" option and get the top thumbnail choices presented by AI. Select the desired thumbnail or click on the "Edit" option to further customize it.

Edit the Selected Thumbnail

In the editing interface, you can add text to the thumbnail using the "Text" tool at the top. Additionally, you have the option to import relevant images with the import image option. After importing, the "Crop" tool allows you to adjust the image as required.

Get Pre-Assembled Video Templates and Edit Them

Filmora V13 also offers to choose predesigned video templates such as food, vlog, music, and much more. Pick a template that aligns with your needs, and the text will get automatically added to your thumbnail. You can further refine the thumbnail with the "Scale," "Position," "Rotation," and "Flip" options. With the "Remove Background" option, you can eliminate the background from the image.

Import Thumbnail From Your Device

If you prefer to import your thumbnail from your device, choose the "From Local" option and click the "+" icon to import the image. You can click on "Edit" to customize the image with text or in your unique style. When you are satisfied with the video, click the "Save" and "Export" options to get the thumbnail next to your video in the system.

AI Text-to-Video

AI Text-to-Video

Are you looking for a video that relates to your topic but lacks enough skills and time to create your own? All you need is a script in this regard because Wondershare Filmora offers text-to-video features with AI support. It can not only turn your text into video but also allow you to edit that video. The AI assistant will help you in creating a perfect script for any topic within seconds.

After getting the video, you can still be able to re-edit the elements from the timeline section. Without any delay, let us show you the process of making AI Text-to-Video quickly.

Discover the AI Text-to-Video Feature in Filmora v13

To begin the process, launch the newer version of Wondershare Filmora in your system. In this regard, head to its official website and download Filmora version 13 for free. Enter the main interface of Filmora V13 and look for the AI Text to Video option there. Once you have clicked on it, you will find another screen to insert your pre-made script.

Set the Preferences and Generate an AI Video

If you don’t have any script, check the "Generated by AI" option to specify the type of text from the drop-down list. Enter your topic name, provide additional details, and click "Generate Text" to proceed.

After adding the script, customize settings like "Text Language" from the available 29 options, choose the "Aspect Ratio," and select an AI voice from the given 6 options. After setting preferences, hit the "Text to Video" option.

Customize and Export as Desired

This way AI will create a video for you in seconds that will contain voiceovers, background music, images, and videos. You can customize the video to make it more relatable to your topic. It can even enable you to change the background music. Finally, you can further edit your project or Export by using the Export button.

AI Smart Masking

AI Smart Masking

AI Smart Masking in Wondershare Filmora means that you can foreground objects and subjects from the background in images and videos. It utilizes advanced AI algorithms to identify objects and generate accurate masks. With the auto-masking function, users can get a smart brush option to create the masking with a single brush. You don’t have to mask the edge of objects with your hands.

Anyone can use this feature to effortlessly mask their images and video. Let’s explore in detail how you can get the maximum out of this feature.

Set Up Filmora 13 in Your System

Download and launch the newer version of Filmora in your system. To do so, visit the official website of Wondershare Filmora and get the Filmora Version 13. Successfully install this software and enter the main home screen to start working on the project.

Access the AI Mask and Use Smart Brush

After importing your project in the timeline, navigate to the "Mask" tool and find the new "AI Brush." It will automatically select the subject and object. You can get a preview if your video has multiple subjects to mask. From the drop-down, you have the option to select the "Smart Brush" to choose an object while excluding other elements.

Customize the Mask by Using AI Mask Panel

Scroll down to find the "AI Mask Panel" and use the "Extend" slider to expand or narrow down the mask. You can also use the "Blur Strength" slider to soften the mask edges and adjust the opacity by using the opacity slider.

To further modify the mask, click on the AI Mask and adjust the mask by selecting its points. Users can track the mask’s movement by tapping the "Path" button and importing another mask through the "Add Mask" option.

AI Mask Applications

Apply AI mask to images that are used to create transitions, 3D texts, background removals, thumbnails, and to emphasize objects. Finally, you have applied the mask and are free to further edit your project or export by using the "Export" button.

AI Vocal Remover

AI Vocal Remover

Many times, people strive to effortlessly remove the vocal part from their video so they can make the dubbing themselves. In this regard, they look for software that can only contain the soundtrack instead of the vocals. Wondershare Filmora introduced an AI Vocal Remover that offers AI intelligent recognition to identify the vocal singing.

It can assist you in getting pure musical accompaniment and separate them automatically. Without any delay, let’s explore how to separate vocals from music in your videos.

Launch the Newer Version of Filmora

To access the AI Vocal Remover, ensure that you have Filmora V13. Find out this latest version from the official website of Wondershare Filmora for free. Launch the application in your system by following a simple process.

Access the AI Vocal Remover and Utilize

Begin with importing music of your choice in the timeline section. There, right-click on the audio and look for the AI Vocal Remover option from the list. AI will start processing and bring two new audio tracks within seconds.

The first track is the vocal version, where AI adds only the vocals of the music. In the second track, you will get the background music that sounds only background when played.

Export Clips as Required

Export the instrumental clip as needed with the audio clip range from 3-10 minutes where you wish to remove vocals.
